Research

Perimeter Institute Quantum Intelligence Lab (PIQuIL) is a research centre and training hub for future leaders at the intersections of artificial intelligence and quantum systems. With partners in academia, industry, and government, the lab leverages the world-class scientists and resources of Perimeter Institute to catalyze leading-edge, multidisciplinary research.
